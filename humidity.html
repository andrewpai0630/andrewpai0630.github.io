Humidity

It looks like there is a correlation between latitude and humidity even though the the relationship doesn't look very strong. The graph shows that the humidity is lower around the arctic and antarctic areas compared to the tropical areas around the equator. This makes sense because the arctic and antarctic areas are usaully cold and dryer. The tropical areas are warm and humid.
