Max Temperature

It looks like there is a correlation between latitude and temperature. Latitude is the distance of a place from earth's equator so the max and min of latitude represents the arctic cirlce and antarctic cirlce. The graph shows that the temperature is the lowest around the min and max of latitude which makes sense because they are the arctic and antarctic areas. The temperature is the highest around the equator (lat=0) which makes sense because the weather around the equator is very tropical.
